2002 QuickBird Imagery

This high-resolution satellite imagery shows the Barrow region as it appeared on August 1st, 2002. It has a pixel resolution of 0.7 m (2.3 ft). The pan-sharpened multispectral QuickBird mosaic was orthorectified by DigitalGlobe, with subsequent processing at the University of Colorado and University of Texas at El Paso. For more information, see http://nsidc.org/data/docs/arcss/arcss304/
