2011 SDMI Spot5 Imagery

This satellite imagery shows the Barrow region as it appeared on June 26th and July 8th, 2011. It has a pixel resolution of 2.5 m (8.2 ft). Pansharpened Spot5 imagery was acquired and processed by the SPOT Image Corporation and CNES as part of the Alaska Statewide Digital Mapping Initiative (SDMI), and as coordinated through Alaska Mapped. Eleven tiles of the SDMI orthomosaic were further processed at the University of Colorado and University of Texas at El Paso to create this orthomosaic.
